About

I am a Research Software Engineer and applied mathematician working in scientific computing, High Performance Computing, and physics-based engineering simulation. I focus on building scalable computational frameworks for real-world industrial and scientific problems.

My work spans development of numerical methods and software for complex multiphysics systems and digital engineering applications.

  • Digital Twins for industrial systems and engineering applications
  • Physics-based simulations in fluid and solid mechanics
  • Finite Element Method (FEM) based numerical frameworks
  • Reduced order modelling and surrogate models
  • Scientific Machine Learning for Partial Differential Equations

I have worked across hydropower engineering, steel manufacturing, and computational physics research environments.
