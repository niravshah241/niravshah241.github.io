About

I am a Research Software Engineer and applied mathematician working at the intersection of scientific computing, engineering simulation, and High Performance Computing.

My work focuses on developing scalable scientific software for:

  • Digital Twins for industrial systems
  • Physics-based engineering simulations
  • Reduced order modelling and surrogate models
  • Scientific Machine Learning for Partial Differential Equations
  • Finite Element Method based numerical methods

I have worked across academia and industry in domains including hydropower engineering, steel manufacturing, and climate and fluid dynamics modelling.
