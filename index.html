About Me

I am an engineer and applied mathematician with experience in scientific software development and high-performance computing. I have experience of working in engineering sectors such as hydropower and steelmaking. My experience includes development of digital twins for industrial and practical applications and open-source software development. I am currently working as Research Software Engineer focusing on development of climate science models implementing parallel computing frameworks, improving software design and software sustainability at the University of Cambridge. Previously, I was Early Stage Researcher under ROMSOC Industrial Doctorate Program. I enjoy developing mathematical approaches for complex problems and creating scientific software which make the utilisation of modern high-performance computing systems simpler.
