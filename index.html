About

I am a Research Software Engineer and applied mathematician working in scientific computing, High Performance Computing, and physics-based engineering simulation.

My work focuses on developing scalable computational methods and software for:

  • Digital Twins for industrial and engineering systems
  • Physics-based simulations in fluid and solid mechanics
  • Finite Element Method (FEM) based numerical frameworks
  • Reduced order modelling and surrogate models
  • Scientific Machine Learning for Partial Differential Equations

I have worked across academia and industry including hydropower engineering, steel manufacturing, and computational physics applications.
